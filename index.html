The Platform

A smarter way to
get feedback before
submission day

PeerReview Hub gives college students a dedicated space to upload essay drafts and receive structured, rubric-driven critiques from peers — fully anonymised to reduce social anxiety and encourage honest, constructive responses.

Explore the Process →
🔒

Anonymous by Design

Submitters and reviewers never see each other's identities — reducing bias and social pressure.

📋

Structured Rubrics

Every review follows a guided rubric — argument, evidence, structure, language — for consistent feedback.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Proactive alerts when your draft is reviewed, plus reminders for pending review tasks.

📊

Progress Dashboard

Track reviews given and received, revision rounds, and personal growth over the semester.

Usability Goals

Built on 7 Measurable
Design Principles

Every design decision traces back to Rogers, Sharp & Preece's seven core usability goals.

01

Effectiveness

Every workflow — submitting, reviewing, receiving feedback — must reach its intended outcome without error.

02

Efficiency

Structured rubric categories reduce decision time during review, streamlining the entire submission process.

03

Safety

Confirmation prompts before submission and undo functionality protect students from accidental data loss.

04

Utility

File upload, feedback forms, notification system, and revision history — tools students actually need.

05

Learnability

Onboarding tooltips and an intuitive interface layout reduce the learning curve for first-time users.

06

Memorability

Consistent icons, labelled navigation, and categorised menus help students recall tasks after weeks away.

07

Satisfaction

Post-review satisfaction ratings and in-platform feedback collection monitor user experience continuously.

UX Design

Desirable Experience
Qualities

💡

Motivating

Progress meters, review counts, and positive submission feedback make reviewing feel meaningful, not laborious.

🌐

Engaging

Guided feedback forms require critical reflection; anonymised community statistics foster a sense of participation.

🤝

Helpful

Contextual cues inside review forms direct feedback at the right moment, guiding reviewers toward quality critique.

🏅

Rewarding

Badges for completed reviews and recognition for consistently useful feedback sustain long-term engagement.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Sociable

Anonymous yet socially aware — shared workspaces and optional group threads extend the platform's academic value.

🚫

Frustration-Free

No overly complex submission flows, no patronising copy, no visually flat interfaces — by explicit design decision.
